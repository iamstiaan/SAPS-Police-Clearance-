Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Clear answers to the most common questions about SAPS police clearance from New Zealand.

How long does SA police clearance take from NZ?

+

Standard processing takes 6–10 weeks from SAPS submission. Fast-track preparation is available for urgent timelines — WhatsApp Elanza to discuss your deadline.

Do I need to travel to South Africa?

+

No. Everything is coordinated from Christchurch. Fingerprints are captured by Elanza personally, and all documentation and submission is handled locally.

Does Elanza take my fingerprints herself?

+

Yes — Elanza is a certified fingerprint technician. She captures your fingerprints personally in Christchurch, and also coordinates booking, documentation preparation, and SAPS submission.

What documents do I need?

+

Typically your South African ID/passport, completed SAPS application form, certified fingerprints, and a cover letter. Elanza sends you a full personalised checklist when you get in touch.

Which countries accept SA Police Clearance?

+

SAPS clearance is accepted for NZ residency, Australia, UK, Canada, UAE, Singapore, and most OECD countries. Elanza advises on specific country format requirements.

Can I get clearance for multiple past addresses?

+

Yes. SAPS clearance covers your full South African criminal record regardless of how many provinces you lived in. One application covers all of them.

What if I have an urgent deadline?

+

WhatsApp Elanza directly with your deadline. She offers fast-track preparation, priority fingerprint scheduling, and expedited submission — SAPS external processing times can't be controlled, but your file gets submitted as fast as possible.

How much does it cost?

+

Pricing depends on the services required. WhatsApp or call Elanza for a transparent quote with no hidden fees. A full service breakdown is provided before you commit to anything.
